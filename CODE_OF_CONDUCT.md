# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive
- Welcome differing viewpoints
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may be reported to [micheletti.emanuele@hotmail.com](mailto:micheletti.emanuele@hotmail.com). All reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential.
